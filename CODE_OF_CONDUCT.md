# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in Dandori a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and constructive in communication
- Accepting and giving feedback professionally
- Focusing on shared project outcomes

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Personal attacks, trolling, or inflammatory behavior
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this policy.

## Reporting

Report incidents to project maintainers via private channels. All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.
